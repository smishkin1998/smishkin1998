OUR STORY - WHY HAVE MEALS DELIVERED?

Between numerous classes and commitments, Miami University students are really busy. So busy, that it is hard to find time to cook a meal that is healthy and quick, or even go to the grocery store to walk up and down aisles for ingredients! Redhawk Recipes was born with a student’s schedule in mind. Cooking and eating healthy can be so simple with our catered meals delivered right to your doorstep. We take care of all of the difficult steps so you can take care of the easy ones.

Eating out at Oxford’s various restaurants all week can be expensive and incredibly unhealthy. Who wouldn’t want a homecooked meal?
